.Neuroplasticity resarch.

The main concept of neuroplasticity refers to the brains ability to adapt. The connections among our neurons inside our brains, can be reorganized in response to our needs, as a result of our relations with different contexts or environments. New learnings stimulate our brains to create new connections between cells. Because of this, we rewire our brains to adapt to new contexts

.Growth mindset.

The concept and belief that intelligence can be develop can be a good definition of "growth mindset". Our mind and intelligence can be improved through studying and hard work, like our muscles when we work out, intelligence can be trained. This is related to our own mindset and volition, being the opposite of a "fixed mindset", that put intelligence and talent as fixed traits
