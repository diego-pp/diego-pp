.Explain a situation where you have had an ethical decision to make. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made and the consequences of the action you took.

When I was 16 years old, I was with my mother, at her friends place. This apartment was on level 14. A big fire started, burning one of the apartments of level 11. All the people between the level 11 and 14 had been desperately running up the stairs, to our apartment, trying to escape from the black and toxic smoke. It was impossible to survive breathing this. After 20 minutes, there were already about 20 people in our level 14 apartment. The last person to come was an old man, upset, saying that his wife was in the middle of the stairs. I was the only young and strong person there. In that situation, I needed to act and think fast, so I put a wet towel on my face, and I found her 2 floors down, on the ground. I carried her on my back, 2 levels up, through the dark smoke (intense, walking blind). I saved her life. She make me a massive cake to say thank you. I think that going through a big fire to save a life, putting myself at risk, was an ethical decision, and our values are truly exposed in a life or death situation, even more if my mom is there, one of the main people of influence in my early days.

I think that the large amount of immigration in my country was a big influence for the whole culture, values and collective identity. We normally are very passional and straight forward, with a culture of hard work. I love NZ, I have been living here for 6 years, but I belong to Argentina. I say that nz is home away from home

.Identify which of your strengths might help you in your learning journey and how they might intersect with learning obstacles.

Architecture is a practical profession, what you design is material, people can see it on the screen, in a model, on paper - they can touch it. Design disciples expose ourselves to others critical opinion. This can be great or not to deal with. Sometimes we can create a beautiful project or things that are not that nice, and constantly need to be modified and improved. This is the design process. I’m beginner 100%, but I’m sure that is the same in web development. I have been through that experience and this can be a little help for me. At the same time this can be a big weakness. Particularly to compare the result of my current web developer tools and status. The functional or aesthetic product that I can do today in this new field is a very low level, and this can be frustrating.

.Share an example from your experience of where you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss the strategies you used at that time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now, and why..

I have been working for 3 years in an architecture office in Argentina. One of my teachers from uni offered me that job. At that stage I was in the 4th year, one away from graduation. One of our workmates was an older woman, with a lot of professional experience and technical knowledge, but a horrendous, selfish human being. She never finished the degree, she owed a couple of final assessments. I think that she wasn’t feeling confident to go, because of her age. That insecurity was always present with her, but transformed in passive aggression to the other people from the office, hiding information or been nasty in many different ways. Unfortunately we had been dealing with her presence during those years. I feel a bit bad talking about this, because she passed away. Mainly the problem was the main boss, but she was productive in different aspects, and to fire her would cost a lot of money. The strategies for this case was a lot of social interaction, we tried to include her. I also shared my knowledge with her (I was working in 3d software and she 2d). Unfortunately though, everything was a short term solution.
