.What is Emotional Intelligence?.

Emotional intelligence can be define as the intellectual or emotional ability to manage and understand one’s and other people feelings, emotions, thoughts , sensations, which allows to have a better self control and to support and help the others.

.How is Emotional Intelligence Difference from Intellectual Intelligence?.

IQ is related to a different aspect of our intelligence. Is more like a way to measure the ability in different areas of knowledge, and can be measurable, in terms of productivity or ability of the person. Like “how smart u are”. EQ is related to human understanding and vision. A bit closer to “wisdom” (?)
